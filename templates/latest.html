The Amazing Crowd Guru

What's this?

The Amazing Crowd Guru is here to answer all your questions. Add crowdguru@appspot.com to your Google talk or Jabber friend list, and ask him a question by sending /tellme <question>. In return, he might ask a small favor...

Available commands:

/tellme <question>Ask the Amazing Crowd Guru a question. If he asks you one back, respond by replying with your answer. If you can't answer, don't - or send /askme for another question to answer.
/askmeHave the Amazing Crowd Guru ask you a question. Answer by replying to him.
/helpDisplay a quick rundown of available commands.
